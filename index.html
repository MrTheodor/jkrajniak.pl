About

I am an enthusiastic software developer with the background in physics, interested in multidisciplinary projects. I worked at KU Leuven in Computer Science Department on my PhD project under the supervisor of Giovanni Samaey and Erik Nies. I was involved in developing following tools:

  • tools for performing a multiscale simulation of polymer melts,
  • comprehensive framework for modeling chemical reactions at a coarse-grained scale

in the context of epoxy-based materials, complex chemical reactions at the coarse-grained level like condensation polymerization or ATRP.

Keywords: C++, Python, Boost, OpenMPI, OpenMP, parallel programming, coarse-graining, modelling, polymer simulations, data processing, molecular dynamics, GROMACS, LAMMPS

Contribute to: ESPResSo++, VOTCA, (more)
