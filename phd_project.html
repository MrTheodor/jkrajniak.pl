Multiscale modeling at the soft/hard matter interface: linking nanoscale up to the micro-mesoscale.

The SBO project InterPoCo is a 4-year strategic research project that will lay the foundations for the H-INT-S program by taking a detailed look at the interface in steel fiber composites and self-healing capsules in polymer matrices.
